What is OMUC?

OMUC stands for Online Monitoring of Unauthorized Construction. It is a platform to verify and view authorized and unauthorized construction projects across the city.

How do I verify if a plot is authorized?

You can use the "Search" section on the website to look up plot IDs and view their approval status and documents.

Who maintains the database?

The platform is linked with local municipal authorities who upload and manage the sanctioned construction records.

Can I report unauthorized constructions?

Yes, we are planning to launch a feature soon where citizens can report suspected unauthorized constructions with location and photos.

Is user registration mandatory?

No, general access to data is public. But for uploading, verifying documents, or admin rights, registration is required.
