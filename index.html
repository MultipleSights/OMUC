Welcome to OMUC

Track and verify authorized and unauthorized construction projects across the city of Indore. Our platform provides a comprehensive list of verified, legal constructions while also helping the public report suspicious activities.

OMUC aims to increase transparency and ensure the safety of urban development. With up-to-date details on construction approvals, owners, and relevant documents, our platform helps citizens stay informed and authorities ensure compliance with building regulations.

Whether you're a resident concerned about illegal constructions, a builder verifying the status of your projects, or an authority looking to monitor urban growth, OMUC offers all the resources you need in one place.
